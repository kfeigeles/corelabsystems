Ideas in Form 2D exploration

This project is based around research conducted around wearable technoloy and the intersection between its aesthetic value and useability.

While exploring this topic I became very interested in how technology can be integrated into high fashion beyond just accessories

and what that would look like and how the general public would react.

Through my research I found that many high fashion brands are apprehensive to integrate technology into their garmets because they do not want

to sacrifice the aesthetic appeal and pricepoint. On the other hand, accessories like watches and fair game.

Many fashion brands like Michael Kors, Fossil, and Hermes have integrated their accessory designs with Apple and have created versions of the smart watch.

My 2D exploration is centered around what high fashion would look like "dripping" with technology.

In each of the images below I Photoshopped over an aspect of the womans clothing and replaced it with a piece of technology.

I used various componets of microcontrollers and chip boards to replace handbags, skirts, and tops.
